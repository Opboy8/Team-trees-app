About Team Trees

Team Trees is a global initiative bringing together people of all walks of life to plant trees and restore our planet's forests. Our mission is to combat climate change and habitat loss by planting millions of trees where they're needed most.

Every ₹100 you donate plants one tree, and our network of planting partners ensures each tree is planted properly in an area that benefits from reforestation.
